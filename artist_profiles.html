Women's History Month

Celebrating the Women of African Music — March 2026

Every March, we honor the women whose voices, vision, and courage shaped the sound of a continent and moved the world. From the stages of Lagos to the studios of Johannesburg, from exile to the Grammy podium, these artists carried forward traditions while breaking every boundary placed before them.

This collection highlights some of the pioneering figures in African and diasporic music — artists who used song as resistance, celebration, and bridge. Their stories remind us that the history of popular music is, at its heart, a history of bold women.
